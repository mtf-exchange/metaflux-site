01 — Differentiation

Three pillars.
No marketing fluff.

Most "next-gen" exchanges win on lower fees. We're not interested in that race. MetaFlux competes on capability — market structure, risk infrastructure, and execution-layer power that other on-chain venues don't offer at all.

01 / Microstructure

Order types other chains don't have.

RFQ for size that shouldn't advertise on the book. Frequent batch auctions for markets where fairness beats latency. MEV-resistant matching via threshold-encrypted intent.

  • RFQ + maker quotes
  • Frequent batch auctions (FBA)
  • Threshold-encrypted order flow
  • On-chain market-making primitives
02 / Risk

Capital efficiency without cascade risk.

Cross-asset portfolio margin nets hedged books to a single SPAN-derived number. A T0 yellow-card grace tier gives accounts one block to react before partial liquidation fires.

  • SPAN-style portfolio margin
  • Dynamic risk parameters
  • Tiered liquidation (T0 → T4)
  • Multi-collateral isolation
03 / Execution

An EVM purpose-built for trading.

Parallel transaction execution via optimistic Block-STM. Derivatives-aware EVM precompiles (portfolio margin eval, ADL pricing, RFQ depth, vault NAV). Native cross-chain primitives.

  • Parallel EVM (Block-STM)
  • Derivatives precompiles
  • Native cross-chain calls
  • EIP-712 signing, EVM addresses
02 — Builders

Permissionless market deploy. Real EVM.

Any builder can list a new perpetual or spot market via an on-chain gas auction — no protocol-team gate, no allow-list, no review committee. The protocol arbitrates; you ship the market.

Win an on-chain gas auction, supply the market spec (leverage, maintenance ratio, funding parameters, oracle source), and the market is live next block. Liquidity is your problem; the protocol does the rest.

04 — FAQ

The honest answers.

For deeper coverage, the documentation site at docs.mtf.exchange has the complete API surface, concept catalog, and integration guides. This is just the highlights.

What is MetaFlux, exactly?

An independent L1 derivatives exchange. Its own validator set, its own BFT consensus, its own EVM execution layer. Not a Hyperliquid fork, not a rollup, not a CEX. We borrow the L1 + integrated-EVM architecture pattern, but the implementation is built from first principles and the differentiation is real.

How is it different from Hyperliquid?

Three places. (1) Market microstructure — RFQ, FBA, MEV-resistant matching that HL doesn't offer. (2) Risk — T0 yellow-card grace tier, cross-asset portfolio margin (SPAN), dynamic risk parameters. (3) Execution — parallel EVM via Block-STM plus derivatives-aware precompiles. Same compat surface for traders who don't care about any of this.

When does mainnet launch?

Mainnet timing follows audit and testnet milestones, not calendar dates. The roadmap targets a multi-month testnet phase with phased validator onboarding before mainnet. Subscribe to updates via the docs site.

What does integration look like?

If you already speak Hyperliquid's protocol, change one URL and one chain ID. If you're greenfield, use the MTF-native SDK (TypeScript with WASM-backed crypto for browser performance; Rust SDK for backend). Either way, the EIP-712 envelope is the same, and EVM tooling — MetaMask, Rabby, Ledger, ethers — works unmodified.

How do I become a validator?

Genesis validator recruitment opens ahead of mainnet. Initial set is small (single-digit), expanding through the first year. Reach out via the validators inquiry below; we're looking for operators with on-chain ops experience and geographic diversity.

What bridges do you support?

USDC via Circle CCTP, natively attested. Other assets via a single audited third-party bridge — selection finalised pre-mainnet via a public ADR. We deliberately did not build a monolithic in-house bridge: blast radius reduction.

Why the trans-pride palette?

The flag's blue / pink / white reads cleanly as a brand system and isn't taken by anyone else in this space. It also signals what kind of community we want: technical, welcoming, and uninterested in the dominant aesthetic of the rest of crypto.
